NIER Hack Minigame

The Nier Hack Minigame is the result of a semester's work by members of the Computer Games Design' subject at the University of Technology Sydney. Nier Hack Minigame is a 2D Topdown game. Play as a Nier Hack Minigame to complete the hack.

You have entered the system, controlled the triangular ship, and go to thecore area to complete the hack. Be careful with defense module.
